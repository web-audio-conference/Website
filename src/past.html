The conference began in 2015 at IRCAM and Mozilla in Paris. Since this WAC 2015, international conferences have been held regularly around the world, and continued in 2016 at Georgia Tech in Atlanta, in 2017 at the Centre for Digital Music, Queen Mary University of London in London, and in 2018 at TU Berlin in Berlin.

  • WAC 2015: IRCAM and Mozilla, Paris, France
  • WAC 2016: Georgia Tech, Atlanta, Georgia, USA
  • WAC 2017: Queen Mary University of London, London, United Kingdom
  • WAC 2018: TU Berlin, Berlin, Germany
  • WAC 2019: Norwegian University of Science and Technology, Trondheim, Norway
  • WAC 2021: Online - Universitat Pompeu Fabra and SonoSuite, Barcelona, Spain
