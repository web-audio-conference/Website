This webpage includes the publications and extended abstracts of all the WAC conferences: papers, posters, talks, demos, artworks, performances, keynotes and workshops if applicable.

  • Single peer-review: All the papers and extended abstracts of papers, posters, talks, demos, artworks and performances have been single peer-reviewed by an expert program commitee (with at least 2-3 reviews).
  • Creative Commons license: The WAC papers are licensed under a Creative Commons Attribution 4.0 International License (CC BY 4.0), and the copyright remains to the author.
  • ISSN: The ISSN for the proceedings series is ISSN 2663-5844.
  • Items: Each item has an individual page with the title of the work, author names, abstract, and the bibtex metadata. If exists, a PDF is linked in the individual webpage. If a related video exists, a link to watch the video is linked in the individual page.
  • Contact: If you find any errors in the list, please feel free to email at contactwac2019.ntnu.no
  • Zenodo: All PDFs are archived in Zenodo also.
